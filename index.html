I draw on psychotherapy and Systemic Family Therapy models in my Psychotherapy work.

A Psychodynamic approach allows for exploration of an individual's inner world and their emotional life, while a Systemic approach allows for considerations of a person's context, their family and cultural systems, as well as difficulties that may arise in relationships between people.

I also offer consultation, advice and supervision to professionals and prospective trainees within my private practice. I work for an NHS Adult Mental Health and Psychology Service, alongside my private practice.
