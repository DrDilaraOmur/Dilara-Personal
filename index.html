I offer both long and short term psychotherapy, in person and online.

I draw on Psychodynamic and Systemic Family Therapy models in my psychotherapy work.

A Psychodynamic approach allows for exploration of an individual's inner world and their emotional life, while a Systemic approach allows for considerations of a person's context, their family and cultural systems, as well as difficulties that may arise in relationships between people.

I offer consultation, advice and supervision to profesionals and prospective trainees within my private practice. I work for an NHS Adult Mental Health and Psychology Service alongside my private practice.
