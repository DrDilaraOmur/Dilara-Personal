I offer long-term and short-term psychotherapy, in person and online.

I draw on Psychodynamic and Systemic Family Therapy models in my Psychotherapy work.

A Psychodynamic approach allows for exploration of an individual's inner world and their emotional life, while a Systemic approach allows for considerations of a person's context, their family and cultural systems and difficulties that may arise in relationships between people.
